About Magnadyne

Advancing healthcare innovation through smarter trials.

Magnadyne Solutions combines precision, innovation, and regulatory excellence to redefine how clinical research data is managed. We specialize in transforming clinical research data into actionable insights that accelerate the path to approval.

Headquartered in Atlanta, GA — with offices in Lagos, Nigeria — we operate across North America and Africa, delivering holistic end-to-end clinical trial solutions for pharmaceutical, biotech, and medical device sponsors worldwide.
